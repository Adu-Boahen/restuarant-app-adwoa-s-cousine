About Us

Welcome to Adwoa's Cousin - where tradition meats taste. At Adwoa's Cousin, food is more than a meal...it's a story. Inspired by the warmth of Ghanaian family gatherings, our restaurant brings together the authentic flavors, conforting aromas, and rich heritage of local cuisine. Every recipe has roots. - passed down, perfected, and prepared with love.

We serve dishes that remind you of home,meals that bring people together, and flavors that stay with you long after the bite. Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch, a family celebration, or your favourite weekend treat, our goal is simple: to give you a delicious experience built on culture, community and care.
